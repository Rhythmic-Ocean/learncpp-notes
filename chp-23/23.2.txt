23.2 — Composition
  -two classes has composition relation if one of the classes object is a member of other class. eg:
    class Creature
    {
    private:
      std::string m_name;
      Point2D m_location;
    }
    here, Point2D is a part of Creature class, they have part-of relationship and hence composition


  NOTE: composition could mean two thing, the broder compostion or a compostion (or obj composition) that part of the broder composition along side aggregrate. Although when ppl say
    compostion it means the broder one, but we will use the narrower one here 

  the class that's the part of should be awaer about the existence of the class that's taking control of one of it's object
  the object's lifetime should be managed by the class that's encompassing it

  More formal relationship criteris:
    The part (member) is part of the object (class)
    The part (member) can only belong to one object (class) at a time
    The part (member) has its existence managed by the object (class)
    The part (member) does not know about the existence of the object (class)

  The relation unidirection, where whole know about the part but the other doesn't know it's a part of whole
